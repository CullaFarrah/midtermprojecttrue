About Robogals


Robogals Cagayan de Oro is a student run, not-for-profit organization, which aims to engage young female school students in engineering based topics. In 2014, Robogals CDO started as the first chapter of Robogals in Southeast Asia and the Philippines. As of 2018, Robogals CDO remains as the only local chapter in the Philippines.

We are part of the Robogals Global network which has over 30 chapters across Australia,New Zealand, the Philippines, the UK, Canada, the USA, the Middle East, South Africa, Indonesia and Japan. To learn more about Robogals Global, you may visit their website here.

Ultimately, our goal is to increase female enrollment in Science, Technology and Engineering related courses in the country. Hopefully, this will lead to more females taking up careers in aforementioned fields, especially in engineering & technology. Not only will this eventually balance the gender-profile in STEM-related careers, we aspire to inspire the new generation of future Filipino engineers and scientists.
